GameHub is an Android application for browsing video games and checking the latest gaming news from around the world.

Features:
- Browse games by various categories: Popular, Recently Released, Coming Soon, etc.
- Search for games.
- View game details: Summary, Genres, Platforms, Modes, Themes, Player Perspectives, Involved Companies, Similar Games, etc.
- View game media: Screenshots, Videos, Artwork.
- Read the latest gaming news.
- Light and Dark themes.
